# Decentralization, Explained Simply

Most chat apps keep your messages in one company-owned backend.
DecentChat flips that model:

- your identity lives in your seed phrase
- your data is replicated across your participating devices/peers
- sync is peer-to-peer

Think of it like collaborative state replication, not mailbox storage.

## What this gives users

- no single company account needed to exist
- better resilience against single-point outages
- stronger user ownership of identity

## What this does NOT magically solve

- if every copy of history is deleted, it’s gone
- you still need good backup hygiene (seed + active replicas)
