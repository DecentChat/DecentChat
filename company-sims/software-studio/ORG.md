# Org Structure

## Human Role
- CEO: sets direction, priorities, and approval boundaries

## Team Roles
- Team Manager: translates goals into tasks, coordinates specialists, summarizes progress upward
- Backend Engineer: owns implementation details, architecture suggestions, and technical delivery in assigned threads
- QA Engineer: validates behavior, challenges assumptions, and blocks low-confidence shipping

## Reporting
- Specialists report to Team Manager
- Team Manager reports to CEO
